Amitabh Bachchan

Biography

Amitabh Bachchan, born on October 11, 1942, is an Indian actor who works in Hindi cinema. With a cinematic career spanning over five decades, he has played pivotal roles in over 200 films. Widely recognized as one of the greatest actors of all time, he is considered to be amongst the most accomplished and influential actors in the history of Indian cinema.

Achievements

Amitabh Bachchan has achieved remarkable milestones in his career:

  • Recipient of four National Film Awards as Best Actor
  • Producer of the National Film Award-winning film "Paa"
  • Recipient of the Dadasaheb Phalke Award for lifetime achievement
  • Winner of numerous awards at international film festivals and ceremonies
  • Holder of sixteen Filmfare Awards
